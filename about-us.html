About Us

Who we are

Haleakala Lei Tours

Haleakala Lei Tours is ushering and transporting visitors to Maui's pride and popular destination, Haleakala (House of Sun) National Park.

As local residents, we see the beauty of the island in a unique way which we share through an absolute collection of Maui travel contents. We help you find your way to Maui through in-depth articles and engaging stories and allow you to connect with local experts for tips to get the most memorable and meaningful Maui experience.

We make exploring Maui convenient and worry-free than ever. Whether you are planning, booking or in an actual trip to Maui, we look forward to being your guide to this incredible island we call home.

Mission

To provide a unique, fun, reliable, safe, convenient, and efficient transportation services to our customers by sharing in-depth articles and stories of Hawaii's ancient and modern culture and connecting them with Maui's rare and sacred park, the Haleakala National Park.

Vision

As a local operator, we want to grow by offering more and a wide range of unique service tours throughout the County of Maui and be known globally through our customers' experiences and testimonials.

Values

  • Attain Self-Fulfillment
  • Be happy
  • Find contentment
  • Respect Nature
  • Worry-Free
  • Be Mesmerized
  • Be Inspiring
What we believe

The Travel Experience!

We believe that seeing the beauty of Maui will mesmerize and inspire travelers because as local residents, we are fortunate to experience it every day. We know what it means to live in Hawaii and speak Aloha by helping you find joy in your travel experience through the unique vibrancy and hospitality. Maui, truly has a lot to offer to its visitors and we're on a mission to share this with the world.
